About Us

BoboCofi is the place to go to after a long night of studying to the next morning dragging your feet into college, our coffee will be sure to energise your learning.

The story of BoboCofi begins with 3 students of NCI which came up with the entrepreneurial idea during one of their classes when they were exhausted after a long nights study and thought of nothing better but a coffee to spark up their learning, the only problem they had was the lack of places close to college to go to. Since coming up with the idea mid class, Thousands of students have walked through our doors seeking the same remedy to their sleepiness and lack of concentration since 2018

We are proud to be the solution to our fellow students who all recommend us regardless if they are in their first year or graduates into jobs. Here at BoboCofi we take your suggestions to diversify our menu and cater to the tastes of each student, offering them the ideal caffeinated beverage to their needs.
